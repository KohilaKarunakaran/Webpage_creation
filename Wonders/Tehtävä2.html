Did you know?

The main pyramid, El Castillo, has 365 steps, corresponding to the days in a year—a tribute to the Maya's advanced understanding of astronomy.

Did you know?

The Taj Mahal changes colours at different times of the day, appearing pinkish in the morning, milky white in the evening, and golden at night.
